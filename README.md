# Cancer Treatment Navigator

**Free, open-source cancer treatment navigator for humans.**

When someone gets a cancer diagnosis, the information landscape is overwhelming. Standard of care, immunotherapy, targeted therapy, clinical trials — it's scattered across thousands of papers, guidelines, and trial databases. This tool puts it all in one place.

## What It Does

Enter your cancer details — type, stage, mutations, biomarkers — and get a personalized, multi-layer treatment pipeline:

- **Layer 0: Standard of Care** — NCCN-aligned frontline protocols with trial evidence
- **Layer 1: Immunotherapy Channels** — 9 antigen channels mapped to your specific cancer
- **Layer 2: Immune Weapons** — 20+ immune weapon classes matched to your profile
- **Layer 3: TME Modulation** — Tumor microenvironment strategies (hot/cold/desert/excluded)
- **Layer 4: Monitoring** — Liquid biopsy, imaging schedules, response tracking

## Cancer Types Covered (30+)

Breast, Lung (NSCLC/SCLC), Colorectal, Pancreatic, Ovarian, Prostate, Melanoma, Bladder, Liver (HCC), Glioblastoma, Head & Neck, Gastric, Esophageal, Cervical, Endometrial, Thyroid, Kidney (RCC), Cholangiocarcinoma, Mesothelioma, Neuroblastoma, Merkel Cell, AML, ALL, CLL, CML, Multiple Myeloma, DLBCL, Hodgkin Lymphoma, and more.

## Key Features

- **Prior therapy awareness** — Knows when you've already failed a treatment and shows post-progression options
- **Mutation-specific pathways** — EGFR, ALK, BRAF V600E, BRCA1/2, HER2, KRAS G12C, and more
- **TME classification** — Hot, Warm, Cold, Desert, Excluded tumor microenvironment strategies
- **Missing data suggestions** — Don't have all test results? Get guidance on what tests matter most, why, and approximate costs
- **Evidence grading** — Every recommendation tagged A (FDA approved) through E (preclinical)
- **Heme vs solid tumor distinction** — Blood cancers get appropriate strategies, not solid tumor defaults
- **India pricing option** — Toggle for India-specific drug costs and availability

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in any browser — no installation, no server, no account needed
2. Select cancer type and stage
3. Add mutations, biomarkers, prior treatments
4. Click "Generate Treatment Pipeline"
5. Review with your oncologist

## Philosophy

- **Free forever.** No subscriptions, no paywalls, no ads.
- **Open source.** MIT license. Fork it, improve it, share it.
- **No data collection.** Everything runs in your browser. Nothing is sent anywhere.
- **Not medical advice.** This is an information tool to help you have better conversations with your oncologist.
